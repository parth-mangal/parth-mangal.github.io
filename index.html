About Me

Building systems that scale.

I'm a Software Engineer II at Blinkit, where I work on ad-tech infrastructure powering off-platform and on-platform advertising at scale. I build the plumbing — Kafka pipelines, gRPC services, Airflow DAGs, and FastAPI backends — that makes advertising products work reliably for brands advertising on Blinkit.

Previously at Zomato, I worked on Social Ads (off-platform Meta campaigns), dining catalog pipelines, and audience segmentation. Before that at Gameskraft, I engineered the Rewards & Referrals microservices for Pocket52, a real-money poker platform with high-concurrency requirements.

I graduated from IIIT Kota with a BTech in Computer Engineering (2019–2023) and enjoy working at the intersection of data engineering, distributed systems, and product impact.
