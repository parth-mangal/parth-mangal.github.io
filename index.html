About Me

Building systems that scale.

I'm a Backend SDE II at Blinkit, working on ad-tech infrastructure that powers off-platform and on-platform advertising. I build the plumbing — Kafka pipelines, gRPC services, Airflow DAGs, and FastAPI backends — that makes advertising products work reliably at scale.

Previously at Zomato, I built Social Ads (off-platform Meta campaigns), dining catalog pipelines, and audience segmentation systems. Before that at Gameskraft, I engineered high-concurrency Rewards & Referrals microservices for Pocket52, a real-money poker platform.

I graduated from IIIT Kota with a BTech in Computer Engineering (2019–2023). I enjoy working at the intersection of data engineering, distributed systems, and product impact.
